## Learner Profiles

### Librarian
Maria works at a university library and wants to enrich catalogue data by linking library records to Wikidata. She is comfortable with metadata standards but has no prior experience with Wikidata or SPARQL.

### Researcher
David is a humanities researcher who wants to make his publications and research data more findable by adding them to Wikidata. He has basic digital literacy but no structured data background.

### Research Data Manager
Priya supports researchers in making their data FAIR. She wants to understand how Wikidata can serve as a hub for persistent identifiers like ORCID and DOI, and how to query it for reporting purposes.

### Open Data Enthusiast
Jonas contributes to Wikipedia and wants to extend his work to Wikidata. He is familiar with the wiki environment but wants to learn the structured data model and how to write SPARQL queries.
